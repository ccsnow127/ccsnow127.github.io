Hello!👋 I am currently an MPhil student in Computer Science and Engineering at the  `Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen`. I have the privilege of working in the `CUSE Laboratory` under the co-supervision of `Prof. Pinjia He` and Prof. `Xiaoying Tang`. My research focuses on the application of Large Language Models to various tasks in software engineering, especially for comment analysis in smart contracts and automatic program repair. Most recently, I have been interested in studying cognition biases in Large Language Models, taking inspiration from psychological principles. 

I am thrilled to share that I have been accepted into the Ph.D. program in Computer Science at `Virginia Tech` (**Go Hokies!**😆). I will be joining the `Seclab`, where I will be very fortunate to receive guidance from `Prof. Peng Gao`. My research at Virginia Tech will focus on Trustworthy AI, specifically addressing the robustness, privacy, and explainability of NLP and GNNs. Additionally, I will explore the security and privacy aspects of LLMs and chatbots. By investigating these areas, I aim to contribute to the development of AI systems that are reliable, secure, and ethically responsible.
