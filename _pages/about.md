I am a PhD student in the `Computer Science` department at `Virginia Tech`, fortunate to be advised by [Prof. Gao](https://people.cs.vt.edu/penggao/). I am broadly interested in the application of large language models in boosting various cybersecurity tasks, such as cyber threat intelligence anlysis, vulnerability discovery, and adversarial attack and defense. I am also interested in the intersection of machine learning and cybersecurity, particularly in the context of adversarial machine learning and privacy-preserving machine learning. You can find more about my research interests [here](https://chengyutong2049.github.io/).

I am currently working on the projects of llm-based `adversarial attack agents`, and application of LLMs to dig out vulnerabilities in `smart contracts`. If you are interested, please feel free to reach out to me. 🥰
