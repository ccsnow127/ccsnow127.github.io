About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate of Computer Science at Virginia Tech, advised by Prof. Peng Gao. I received my B.Eng. in Software Engineering from Beijing Jiaotong University. My research mainly focuses on the following topics:

  • Automated and AI-assisted security operations (SecOps)
  • LLM/MLLM specialization and customization
  • Agentic planning, reasoning, and learning
